Biography



Hey, my name is Aayush. I'm a guitarist, wintertime skier, and an unshakable technology enthusiast. I spent the first decade of my childhood living in Melbourne, Australia, before my family decided to immigrate here, to Winnipeg, in 2015, where I have resided ever since.

I have always been interested in the ins and outs of technology. As a child, I was always fascinated by cool gadgets I would see, and that passion has continued to the present date. Technology has so many uses. It is used by people worldwide to stay connected to one another, it's used by many people to help keep their lives organized, and much more. I also find it fascinating how so many technological services are now readily accessible in most smartphones that have become essential parts of many of our lives.

I like to explore different activities, but some hobbies that have stuck to me include playing the guitar and skiing. I have been playing the guitar intermittently since I was ten years old, but I only began to play more consistently last year. You may be thinking, how could you possibly ski in a place like Australia? In the South, snow was a rare sight. To experience snow, you would have to drive for hours up steep mountains, but every time I would go on a mountainous road trip with my family, I would make sure to ski for hours. After moving to Winnipeg, I was thrilled to have snow essentially everywhere, and I improved my skiing skills heavily.
